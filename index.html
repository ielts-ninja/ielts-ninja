While doing reading task you must know how to read passage. It is not only matching keywords everywhere and and searching data according to questions. You must understand the topic first of all.

Tip: Read the beginning of the text before you read questions (first 4 lines or first paragraph). It helps you to understand the main meaning of the topic.

You know that it is much more easier go downhill skiing from Mount Everest than climb up. So don't start from bottom, start from the peak of the mountain.

That's clear, go forward. Let's see small part of the topic:

Situated in Nepal, Himalayan Mountains, where Mount Everest located, is very beautiful place to visit. However, beauty of people's life in country, even in it's capital Catmando, is not so impressive, even bad.

Questions: choose TRUE or FALSE
  1. Mount Everest located in Nepal, near Himalayan Mountains
  2. Nepalenes' life is very beautiful, expect of Catmando
Okay before you begin looking for answer, you must know the theme of topic. This text about Nepal, not mainly about mountains. Here you need not to search where keywords hidden. It is part of text, taken especially for these two questions. Consider it as that the part of the text with keywords is found, and you must understand this part fully.

Tip: Separate each sentence in following scheme:
  1. Match Subjects of sentence
  2. Match their definitions (for each subject)
  3. Match verbs for each subject (they could be hidden in context or they are verbs "am" "is" "are")
  4. If exists, match objects for each verb


You should get structure tree of the sentence such as:
  • Subject1 (definition1) (definition2) :
    • Verb1:
      • Object1
  • Subject2 (definition) :
    • Verb1:
      • Object1
      • Object2
    • Verb2:
      • Object1
We try to do that with first sentence:
Subject of this sentence exactly is "Himalayan Mountains", and his descriptions are "Situated in Nepal" and "where Mount Everest located". Verb there is "is". Is what? "beautiful place to visit" scheme is following:

    • is
      • very beautiful place to visit

    • is
